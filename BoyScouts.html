BOY SCOUTS 

Nick Romero (Boy Scout Master) has countless years of experience in Scouting. He has earned his eagle scout rank, the highest rank you can possibly get in scouting. He is passionate about every child exceeding in the scouting program.

Boy Scouts Info

Your child can join Boy Scouts from the ages of 5-18. After completing 18 years you and your child can continue to be in the program (after earning eagle scout rank or whatever rank he was left on) but only to help with activities. Parents are also welcome to help with the activities.
