Rank Information

This is some information on some of the ranks in the Scouting Program.

Cub Scouts Ranks
  • Bobcat- All Scouts start as Bobcats, no matter what age
  • Tiger Cub- First graders or 7-year-olds
  • Wolf- Finished first graders or  8-year-olds
  • Bear-  Finished second grade or  9-year-olds
  • Webelos one-  Finished Third graders or are 10-years-old
  • Arrow of Light- Finished fourth grader or 11-year-old
